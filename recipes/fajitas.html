Fajitas Recipe

Description

Let's get cooking with some seriously tasty fajitas. All you need is some chicken, peppers, cheese, sour cream and some spices to make this tasty dish

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast cut into strips
  • 1 red pepper, 1 green pepper and 2 jalepenos
  • Spices to season

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into strips and then place in lightly oiled pan on high heat
  2. Add seasoning and cook chicken until almost done
  3. Remove chicken and add peppers to pan
  4. Cook peppers until soft and then add chicken back with more seasoning
