Steak Recipe

Description

Fire up the grill and make some tasty steak. This recipe comes together quickly and and will be sure to please. All it requires is some salt, pepper and a piece of delectable meat. OH YEAH!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Other spices to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to high temperature
  2. Season steak with salt, pepper and other spices as desired
  3. Grill steak until it reaches desired level of doneness, flipping regularly
